Shower Thoughts Poetry

by Asisipho Msitho

Posted on August 22, 2020 at 12 PM

I call most of my poetry shower thoughts poetry because for most of them, their biggest punchlines came to me during those intimate, quite moments of self-care where my mind felt safe to wander. They deliver the punch because they are as raw and naked as I was in the moments they can to be, it is said that the poem you are most afraid to write is the one you should definitely write. I think sometimes some peices just ooze out of you, even when you are not ready to deliver them because they are ready to be told.
